Accessibility Compliance

WCAG Compliance Summary

  • AA Standard (4.5:1): All text combinations meet or exceed this requirement
  • AAA Standard (7:1): Most combinations meet this higher standard
  • Color Independence: Information is not conveyed by color alone
  • Focus Indicators: Interactive elements have clear focus states
  • High Contrast: Maximum contrast ratios for critical information
